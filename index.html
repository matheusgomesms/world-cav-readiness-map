CAV Readiness Map

This map is a tool to increase the visibility of road infrastructure (or lack thereof) in cities around the world, providing data to assist in public policy advocacy.

Utilizing data from our global study, this open-source platform aims to engage the community in understanding and improving urban mobility for the new era of Connected and Autonomous Vehicles (CAVs).
